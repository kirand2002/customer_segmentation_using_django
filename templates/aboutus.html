What is Customer Segmentation?

Customer segmentation is the process of dividing a customer base into groups based on common characteristics. This helps businesses understand customer needs and target them more effectively.

  • Demographic Segmentation: Age, gender, income, occupation.
  • Behavioral Segmentation: Purchase behavior, brand interactions.
  • Psychographic Segmentation: Lifestyle, interests, values.
  • Geographic Segmentation: Location-based targeting.
